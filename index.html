RIGOROUS SELF CRITIQUE

Stopping the war within.

01

Introduction

This virtual zine details the ways war integrates itself within our daily lives and how we can combat against it. This topic was chosen after asking myself "how could someone willingly take part in these atrocities?" This lead me to exploring how civilian life can be complicit in war efforts, whether knowingly or unknowingly. In fact, I would say that in most cases, the greatest evils are not from direct combatants, but from the civil society that supports it.
Mural of War by Gari Melchers.
